About Force Arcadium

The Force Arcadium is a multi-game community currently serving gamers across various titles. Hierarchically organized under bigger factions of 'Legions' and smaller 'Branches', we are players gathered together under a single banner to game and build lasting friendships. Join us in this exciting gaming adventure and meet amazing people!

Our Divisions

Following are our various divisons. More divisions coming soon. Hover over the images to unveil the secrets.

Club Penguin

Help Force is an active Club Penguin army which regularly hosts stamp events, army battles and several tournaments. We also tracks mascots that come online, and have a lot in the store for you!

Minecraft

A Minecraft minigame server that features a new minigame every week, plus a weekly game event. There are currently 200+ members in our Minecraft community.

Among Us

If you're red, you're the impostor. Join us in the weekly Among Us game nights and have fun bluffing the crewmates or finding the imposter among us!

Krunker

Join us in our Krunker.io adventure as we roam around several maps trying to get those sneaky headshots. We also conduct tournaments with a lot of rewards for the winning teams!

Supercell

The Force Arcadium's Supercell Division codenamed Armada is where every Supercell players who play across various games such as Brawl Stars, Clash of Clan, Clash Royale come together and engage in all-round activities.

Roblox

The Force Arcadium's Roblox Division is where fellow Roblox players get together to play various games of different genres on Roblox such as Survival, Shooters, Strategy and much more.
